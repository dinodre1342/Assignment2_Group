Shipping and Delivery

  • Deliveries are only subjected within Singapore only.

  • After order has been placed, delivery will take 2 - 5 days to arrive at destination.

  • There are no express delivery or one - day delivered after purchased for this service.

  • Our professional courier will send a message to confirm delivery in 30 minutes within arrival.

Exchange and Returns

  • If user receives (upon arrival) purchased product(s) in broken sealed, faulty, or cosmetic damages; please do not hesitate to let us know and we will send a new parcels with free of charge. Customers are able to request for exchange within 3 days after products have been purchased.

  • Warranty is covered for 1 year but it does only apply to Fish Tank Products Catalog.

  • For live products catalog (e.g Fresh Water Fish, Salt Water Fish, or Puppies), customers feel free to exchange if they do not desire to keep their purchased pets within 1 day after arrival.

  • For Return Products, we recommend you to use Singpost. Currently, we have collaboration with Singpost. If you are using other couriers, please provide full information about the parcel.
