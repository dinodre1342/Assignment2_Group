Shipping and Delivery

  • Deliveries are only subjected within Singapore only.

  • After order has been placed, delivery will take 2 - 5 days to arrive at destination.

  • There are no express delivery or one - day delivered after purchased for this service.

  • Our professional courier will send a message to confirm delivery in 30 minutes within arrival.

  • If there is not someone around at destination, we will immediate bring the parcel back to our warehouse. Our professional customer service will call you to make another appointment for delivery or pick-up time.

  • Our customers will only be given 3 days for the parcel(s) needs to be delivered or pick-up. If customer has still not received or picked up, our parcel will return back to our store and money will be refund to our rightfully customer. Refund may take up to 20 days depending on the bank credibilitya and reliability.

Exchange and Returns

  • If user receives (upon arrival) purchased product(s) in broken sealed, faulty, or cosmetic damages; please do not hesitate to let us know and we will send a new parcels with free of charge. Customers are able to request for exchange within 3 days after products have been purchased.

  • Warranty is covered for 1 year but it does only apply to Fish Tank Products Catalog.

  • For live products catalog (e.g Fresh Water Fish, Salt Water Fish, or Puppies), customers feel free to exchange if they do not desire to keep their purchased pets within 1 day after arrival. Refund does not apply on this term.

  • For Return Products, we recommend you to use Singpost. Currently, we have collaboration with Singpost. If you are using other couriers, please provide full information about the parcel.

  • Refund is available for Fish Tank only. The refund time may take up to 20 days depending on bank credibility and reliability.
